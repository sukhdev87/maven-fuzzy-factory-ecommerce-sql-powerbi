# 🛒 Maven Fuzzy Factory E-Commerce Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the performance of an e-commerce business using SQL Server and Power BI. The objective was to evaluate website traffic, customer conversion behavior, product performance, and refund trends to uncover actionable business insights.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow including data cleaning, SQL analysis, KPI development, dashboard creation, and business recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze website traffic sources and customer acquisition channels.
* Measure conversion performance across campaigns and devices.
* Evaluate product sales, revenue contribution, and cross-sell opportunities.
* Identify refund trends and their impact on profitability.
* Provide data-driven recommendations to improve business performance.

## 📈 Key Insights

### Traffic & Conversion

* Gsearch generated 66% of total website sessions.
* Organic traffic accounted for approximately 18% of sessions.
* Mobile users demonstrated a higher repeat session rate than desktop users.
* Brand campaigns achieved the highest conversion rates.
* Overall conversion rate reached 6.83%, outperforming common e-commerce benchmarks.

### Revenue & Products

* Original Mr. Fuzzy generated the highest sales volume and revenue.
* Forever Love Bear ranked second in both sales and revenue contribution.
* Hudson River Mini Bear recorded the highest cross-sell rate.
* Revenue increased consistently year-over-year.
* December was the highest revenue-generating month.

### Refund Analysis

* Overall return rate remained manageable at 4.32%.
* Birthday Sugar Panda recorded the highest return rate.
* Original Mr. Fuzzy generated the highest refund loss due to its large sales volume.
* Hudson River Mini Bear demonstrated the lowest return rate and refund impact.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Reduce dependency on Gsearch by expanding SEO and alternative acquisition channels.
* Increase mobile-focused retention initiatives.
* Leverage Hudson River Mini Bear in bundle and cross-sell campaigns.
* Investigate the causes of high returns for Birthday Sugar Panda.
* Closely monitor Original Mr. Fuzzy returns due to their financial impact.
* Increase inventory and marketing efforts before the holiday season.
